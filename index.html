计算原理：

设输入的两点坐标为$$ (\frac{a}{b},\frac{c}{d}), (\frac{e}{f},\frac{g}{h}) $$

直线解析式为$$ y=mx+n $$

将两点坐标代入解析式，得$$ \left\{ \begin{aligned} \frac{a}{b}m+n=\frac{c}{d} -- I \\ \frac{e}{f}m+n=\frac{g}{h} -- II \end{aligned} \right. $$

I - II 得 $$ (\frac{a}{b}-\frac{e}{f})m=(\frac{c}{d}-\frac{g}{h}) $$

$$ \begin{equation} \begin{split} m &=(\frac{c}{d}-\frac{g}{h})÷(\frac{a}{b}-\frac{e}{f}) \\ &=\frac{ch-dg}{dh}÷\frac{af-be}{bf} \\ &=\frac{ch-dg}{dh}·\frac{bf}{af-be} \\ &=\frac{bf(ch-dg)}{dh(af-be)} \end{split} \end{equation} $$

将上式代入I得 $$ \frac{a}{b}(\frac{bf(ch-dg)}{dh(af-be)})+n = \frac{c}{d} $$

即 $$ \frac{abf(ch-dg)}{bdh(af-be)}+n = \frac{c}{d} $$

$$ \begin{equation} \begin{split} n &=\frac{c}{d}-\frac{abf(ch-dg)}{bdh(af-be)} \\ &=\frac{bcdh(af-be)-abdf(ch-dg)}{bd^2h(af-be)} \end{split} \end{equation} $$

故直线解析式为：$$ y=\frac{bf(ch-dg)}{dh(af-be)}x+\frac{bcdh(af-be)-abdf(ch-dg)}{bd^2h(af-be)} $$
